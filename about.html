Philip Steininger is a self-taught web developer that specializes in object-oriented programming with five years of experience working in science and technology. He has taken coursework at Harvard University and is currently enrolled at UC Davis extension to strengthen his foundations. In his work, he strives for efficiency, clean aesthetics, and a streamlined design.

Previous employers have lauded Philip’s strengths in logical thinking, attention to detail, and thoroughness throughout all of his work. These qualities shine through in his web development in his ability to create clean, efficient code, and thoroughly debug his work.

For fun, Philip greatly enjoys solving puzzles and completing challenges. He is enthusiastic about playing board games that are centered around logical reasoning and strategic planning.
